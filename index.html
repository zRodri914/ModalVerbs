DEFINICION:

Son verbos auxiliares que se utilizan junto a un verbo principal para expresar funciones tales como pedir permiso o expresar prohibición.

VERBOS:

CAN / PODER

Neg: can not / can't

Ej: Luffy can stretch his arms like rubber.

COULD / PODRIA

Neg: could not / couldn't

Ej: Nami could navigate through any storm.

MAY / PUEDE QUE

Neg: may not / mayn't

Ej: The Straw Hat crew may discover a mysterious island.

MIGHT / PUEDE QUE

Neg: might not / mightn't

Ej: Sanji might cook a delicious meal for the crew.

MUST / DEBER

Neg: must not / mustn't

Ej: Usopp must fix the ship's mast before the next storm.

SHOULD / DEBERIA

Neg: should not / shouldn't

Ej: Franky should upgrade the Thousand Sunny for the Grand Line.

OUGHT TO / DEBERIA

Neg: ought not to / oughtn't to

Ej: The crew ought to trust each other in times of peril.

SHALL / DEBERIA

Neg: shall not / shan't

Ej: We shall conquer the New World together!

WILL / *futuro

Neg: will not / won't

Ej: Luffy will become the Pirate King.

7) NO PUEDEN SER CONJUGADOS EN TODOS LOS TIEMPOS VERBALES
